About Me

This is a website that I am developing to learn more about web development and design. I have over 10 years of experience in library related information technology of all types. My work has mainly revolved around integrated library system administration, including Sierra, Millennium, Symphony, and Polaris. One of my goals in learning these new skills is to apply them to work applications.

I am currently attending Epicodus, a computer programming trade school in Portland, OR, with a focus in Ruby on Rails and Javascript. If you want to know more about my professional background, please feel free in click on the LinkedIn logo in the footer of every page to go to my LinkedIn profile. For examples of the projects that I have done, you can go to the projects tab on this site or use the GitHub link in the footer to see the code of all my saved projects.

When not working with library information technology, my interests include all things sports Michigan related, visiting new places, and spending time with my wife Melanie along with our two kitties Monty and Lily.
